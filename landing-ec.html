What is CampusMap?

CampusMap is a student-made interface designed to aid newcoming students. By entering a school name into the search bar, you will be able to view and interact with your new school with additional information for classes.

Although maps are limited, one will be able to add maps upon registration. If you have any questions, feel free to ask by clicking the contact information below.

Login or register to add your own maps.
