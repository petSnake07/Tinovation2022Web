ABOUT US

CampusMap

CampusMap is a student-led project developed by the Tinovation team at Cupertino High School. CampusMap offers students a new perspective of their schools, enables newcomers to understand how to go from place to place, and serves as a collective hub for school-related map info. CampusMap is a free tool that can be used with just an account.

Tinovation

Tinovation is a student-led club at Cupertino High School in Cupertino, CA. Tinovation leaders spend one semester teaching members about programming and one semester developing a product. Tinovation officers help members gain a deeper understanding of various programming and markup languages. Tinovation is the school's largest CS club and helps run programming competitions and semester-long projects.
